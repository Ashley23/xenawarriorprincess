Welcome to Alexandra

So what is Walking Dead? The short answer is that it is a very difficult and dangerous world to live in. You have zombies and people to worry about, something we hope we're doing constantly. A slightly longer version is that the government was working on something in the CDC Lab in Atlanta and ended up creating a virus that causes the dead to rise and eat people.

So... what aren't we?

We do what we have to in order to survive. We can be considered killers, monsters, or even untrustable.

No one will ever get in our way or stop us from getting what we want/need we have been through too much to give up or be stopped now! Do not get in our way!
